# 📊 Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis (EDA)

## 📝 Overview
This project is an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a bank's telemarketing campaign. The goal of this project is to act as a "data detective," diving into over 45,000 client records to uncover trends in customer demographics, financial standing, and how they interact with the bank's marketing efforts. 

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Data Manipulation:** Pandas
* **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **Environment:** Google Colab / Jupyter Notebook

## 📂 The Dataset
The dataset (`banking_data.csv`) contains client information collected during a direct phone call marketing campaign. It includes categorical data (like jobs and education) and numerical data (like age and bank balance).

## 🔍 Key Questions & Insights Explored
During this analysis, we used data visualization (Histograms, Bar Charts, Boxplots, and Pie Charts) to answer the following business questions:

**Demographics:**
1. What is the age distribution of the clients?
2. How does the job type vary among the clients?
3. What is the marital status distribution of the clients?
4. What is the highest level of education among the clients?

**Financial Standing:**
5. What proportion of clients have credit in default?
6. What is the distribution of average yearly balances?
7. How many clients have active housing loans?
8. How many clients have personal loans?

**Campaign Interaction:**
9. What communication types were used during the campaign?
10. What is the distribution of the last contact day of the month?
11. Which months saw the most campaign activity?
12. What is the distribution of the call durations?
13. *(More insights coming soon!)*
